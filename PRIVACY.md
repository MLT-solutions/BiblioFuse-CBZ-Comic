
# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2026

**BiblioFuse: Comic Binder** is a local desktop utility.

1.  **No Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal files, comic books, or metadata. All processing occurs physically on your computer's hard drive.
2.  **No Internet Usage:** The application functions 100% offline. It does not phone home.
3.  **File System Access:** The app requires read/write access to the folders you explicitly select to perform conversion tasks.
4.  **Third Party Libraries:** We use `Qt6` (GUI), `Pillow` (Image Processing), and `pypdfium2` (PDF Rendering). These are trusted open-source libraries running locally.
